About Us

Bridging the communication gap between spoken language and sign language through innovation.

About the Project

Speech to Sign Animation Converter is an innovative technology that bridges the communication gap between spoken language and sign language. Using advanced AI and real-time processing, our tool converts speech into accurate and fluid sign language animations, making communication more accessible for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community.

Why We Created This

Millions of people around the world face communication barriers due to hearing impairments. Traditional sign language interpretation is not always available, creating a challenge in workplaces, schools, and public settings. We recognized this problem and wanted to create a smart, AI-driven solution that allows for seamless interaction between the hearing and non-hearing communities.

Who We Are

We are a team of developers, designers, and accessibility advocates passionate about bridging the communication divide through technology. Our goal is to make sign language translation easy, intuitive, and accessible to everyone, empowering people with the ability to connect without limitations.

Our Mission

To provide real-time, AI-driven sign language translation that enables effective communication for individuals with hearing impairments, making the world a more inclusive place.

Our Vision

We envision a future where speech-to-sign technology is widely adopted in schools, businesses, and public spaces, allowing for universal accessibility and seamless communication.
